Key Contribution

  • Developed the Companion AI system, which includes Player AI for Classic mode.
  • Developed combat gameplay features.


    • Companion AI - The core design of the combat involves allowing the player to give companion NPC orders or switch to play as them at any time during combat. Therefore, the AI system is designed to predict the player's intentions and provide the right support at the best timing, making the player feel like they are controlling each member of the party. The structure includes an AI director handling high-level decisions for each companion NPC, an AI controller, and a behavior tree managing basic states and actions. Additionally, custom EQS is implemented to provide the move target, the attack target, and handle dynamic obstacle avoidance.
