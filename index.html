About me

I presently work as a gameplay programmer at Ubisoft Quebec. With over a decade of expertise as a game engineer, I specialize in developing gameplay, AI, and animation technology. If you're keen on exploring the projects I've contributed to, you can find them here .

My journey into game development began at the age of 14 when I created my first game using the Age of Empires editor. It was incredibly exciting not only to construct the game but also to witness others enjoy playing it. I have a strong passion for learning every aspect of game development, ranging from programming and game design to project management and art. This passion has led me across various countries, from Taiwan to Japan, the US, and now Canada.

Although most of my time is dedicated to my programming day job, I also engage in game jams to apply and refine my game design skills. If you're interested, you can check them out on My Itch.io page.
